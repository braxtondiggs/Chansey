Connecting your exchange lets Cymbit read your portfolio, track your orders, and manage trades on your behalf. We only need API key permissions, and your funds always stay on the exchange.

Your risk profile tells us how aggressively to trade, from conservative strategies that prioritize capital preservation to aggressive ones that chase higher returns. Together with your exchange connection, this lets Cymbit automatically monitor the market, identify opportunities, and manage your positions without you having to watch charts all day.

Opportunity selling automatically sells weak positions to fund stronger buy opportunities. This step is optional and can be configured later in settings.
