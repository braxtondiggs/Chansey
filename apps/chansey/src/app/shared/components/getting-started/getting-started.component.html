Before {{ appName }} starts trading for you, we carefully test every strategy. Here's what happens next:

We test against history
We run your strategy against months of past market data to make sure it has an edge. Takes a few days.
We practice with pretend money
Your strategy trades live markets — but with fake money first. Usually 1 to 4 weeks, depending on how often your strategy trades.
We do a final safety review
If your strategy passes, you go live. If not, we'll tell you and try a different one — no silent waiting.
We'll email you at each milestone
You don't need to watch. We'll reach out when something changes — and your dashboard will always show where you are.
